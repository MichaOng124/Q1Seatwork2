About
Crisostomo Ibarra He is the protagonist of the story. He studied abroad for several years and was exposed to the European ideals of nationalism, democracy, and liberty.
Maria Clara She is a very beautiful woman of wealth. She is speculated to be daughter of Kapitan Tiago.
Rafael Ibarra He is the meztizo father of Crisostomo. He was sent to jail for allegedly killing a Spanish tax collector. He proceeded to die in prison due to depression.
Kapitan Santiago He is a wealthy Filipino who is a good family friend of the Ibarra's. He doesn't respect the Catholic church and merely feigns admiration to preserve his good image in the eyes of the Spaniards.
Father Damaso He thinks of himself as superior to everyone. He reacts emotionally and violently whenever things do not go according to his wishes. He also craves constant respect and ego inflation.
